A thick chicken and vegetable soup in a pie crust


- 5 Tbsp butter
- 1 diced onion
- 1 cup carrots
- 1/2 cup celery
- 1/3 cup flour
- 1 3/4 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 cups shredded chicken
- peas/frozen veggies
- salt and pepper, tabasco hot sauce
- 2 pie crusts
